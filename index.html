About Me

I earned a Master’s in Computer Science concentrated in AI and machine learning from Fordham University, which I attended from August 2018 - May 2020, and I am seeking a full-time software engineering role. I have 4+ years of experience coding in Python (including tools like Pandas and scikit-learn), C#, and C++. I have 2+ years of experience coding in Java and SQL. And, I've recently picked up JavaScript and ReactJS. At Fordham, I was a Graduate Research Assistant for Professor Md. Ruhul Amin, Professor Ying Mao, and Professor Truong-Huy Nguyen. During my time on the robotics team, I had the privilege to have Ajmal Aqtash as my mentor and friend.
